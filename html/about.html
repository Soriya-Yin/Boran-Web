Our Purpose

Preserving Khmer Culture

The purpose behind building this website is to share the richness of Khmer culture with everyone who wants to discover and learn from our Khmer heritage. This platform aims to provide clear, reliable information and trustworthy documentation about our shared cultural wealth. Through this project, we hope to inspire younger generations to appreciate, preserve, and promote Cambodian traditions for the future.
